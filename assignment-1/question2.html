Easy Fried Rice

Summary

Fried rice has been a staple breakfast food for many asians. It is made using leftover rice from the previous night's dinner and cooked with an assortment of ingredients commonly found in the kitchen. Nowadays, one can find all ranges of fried rice. From a simple egg fried rice to the more fancier ones such as lobster fried rice.

Ingredients

  • Leftover rice
  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • Shallots
  • Eggs
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Salt
  • White pepper

Directions

  1. Heat a wok and pour the oil in.
  2. Once the oil heats up, put in the garlic and shallots. Stir as not to burn them.
  3. When the fragrance of the garlic and shallots starts to emerge, put in the rice and mix it together. keep stirring so as not to burn the rice.
  4. Once the rice has been coated evenly, push it aside in the wok to make space for the eggs.
  5. Pour the eggs in the wok and scramble them to your liking.
  6. Once scrambled, mix the eggs and rice together.
  7. Season to taste.
  8. Serve
